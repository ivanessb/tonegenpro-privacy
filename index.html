ToneGenPro – Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2025

ToneGenPro ("the App") is a professional audio tone generator developed by Ioan Florucza ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data the App collects, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Advertising Data

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Approximate location (IP-based, not GPS)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App uses the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalised ads, in compliance with the GDPR. You may change your consent choice at any time from the About → Privacy Settings menu inside the App.

1.2 In-App Purchases

Purchases (e.g. "Remove Ads", "Pro") are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment information. Google may share a purchase token and product ID with the App solely to unlock the purchased features.

1.3 Locally Stored Preferences

The App stores your settings, presets, and usage counters (e.g. session count for the rating prompt) on-device using the platform's Preferences API. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

1.4 Crash & Diagnostic Data

The App does not integrate any third-party crash reporting or analytics SDK. Diagnostic information (e.g. stack traces) is only written to the local debug log and is not transmitted.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Precise location (GPS)
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Audio recordings — all audio is generated and played or exported locally
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob – ad serving
  • Google Play Billing – in-app purchases
  • Google User Messaging Platform – GDPR consent
  • Google Play In-App Review – rating prompt

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All locally stored data (settings, presets) can be deleted by clearing the App's data or uninstalling it. We do not retain any data on our servers. For data collected by Google AdMob or Google Play, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights (EEA / GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Withdraw ad personalisation consent at any time (About → Privacy Settings)
  • Request information about data processing
  • Request deletion of any personal data we may hold

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
